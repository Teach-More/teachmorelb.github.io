About TeachMore

Learning that actually connects

TeachMore is an education agency built on a simple idea: the right match between a student and a teacher unlocks real progress. We bridge the gap between learners and educators across Lebanon.

From matching families with private tutors to running structured exam-prep courses, we obsess over quality, fit, and outcomes.

Vetted educators, every teacher is screened for expertise and teaching quality.
Outcome-focused, programmes built around exams, grades, and real goals.
Always reachable, quick, human support over WhatsApp.
Trusted by families & students Across schools and universities in Lebanon
