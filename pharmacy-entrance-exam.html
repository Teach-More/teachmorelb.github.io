Course overview

Everything you need, in one focused programme

The pharmacy entrance exam rewards students who understand the fundamentals deeply and can apply them quickly under time pressure. This course is engineered for exactly that. We rebuild your foundations in chemistry and biology, sharpen your quantitative reasoning, and drill exam technique until it becomes second nature.

Every week blends concept teaching with active practice: worked problems, past-paper style questions, and timed sets. You'll always know where you stand and what to work on next, with direct feedback from your instructor.

Whether you're starting from scratch or polishing weak spots, the programme adapts to your level and gets you exam-ready.

What you'll gain

  • Mastery of the core chemistry & biology tested
  • Fast, reliable quantitative problem-solving
  • Proven strategies for pacing and multiple-choice
  • Full-length mock exams under real conditions
  • A personalised study plan and weekly feedback
